Branding & Store Listing

Suggested store listing copy (short):
"YotoLite — simple NFC audio cards for kids. Create, share, and play audio cards with family." 

Suggested long description:
"YotoLite is a kid-friendly NFC-powered audio player. Create audio cards from your computer or within the app, tap a card to play stories and songs, and share with family members using secure cloud sharing. Works offline and online."

Assets:
- App icon (512x512) and adaptive icons
- Screenshots of Play UI, Create Card flow, and Family Sharing screen

Kid-mode considerations:
- No external links without parental consent
- Clear privacy information and parental controls

Store assets checklist:
- High-res icon, feature graphic (if Play Store), 4–8 screenshots, promotional video (optional)
